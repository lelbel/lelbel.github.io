Overview

About

Walk to the Ocean! Professionally cleaned, expansive home with room for everyone! 6 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms, 2 full kitchens, 2 living rooms, game room, balconies with seating and ocean views. Large fenced backyard that features a grill and plenty of privacy. Perfect for cooking out and spending time with family and friends! Other amenities include backyard games, 2 outdoor showers, beach towels, beach chairs and a beach cart.

The Space

Coastal relaxation at its best! Sparkling clean beach house! Cleaning fee includes bed linens and towel sets for each guest. Relax more and pack less! *SUMMER 2024 Salt and Sand rents Saturday to Saturday for 7 days

Guest Access

Our guests have access to the entire house and backyard. There is a huge driveway that offers plenty of parking. No more than 8 cars and please do not park under the house.

Other Things to Note

*SUMMER 2024 Salt and Sand will book Saturday to Saturday with a 7 night minimum stay.
