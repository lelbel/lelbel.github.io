How Reviews are Written


At Game Review Hub, we evaluate each game using our unique "Gamer Points" system. This comprehensive scoring method assesses multiple aspects of the game, including graphics, gameplay, story, and replay value. Each category is rated out of 10 Gamer Points, and the total score provides an overall rating out of 40. This approach ensures a balanced and thorough review, helping gamers make well-informed choices about their next adventure.
