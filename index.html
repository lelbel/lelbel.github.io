Level Down is a fast-paced game that blends parkour, agility, and quick thinking. Move around a hexagonal grid where each step causes the floor to fall away beneath you. Navigate through various floors as you climb upwards in a quest to get to the top. Shell Shocked

Shell Shocked is a rage-style platformer in which you play as a Hermit Crab. Dodge Umbrellas, avoid pitfalls, and outsmart Seagulls to survive. Make your way to the sand castle to win! This game was created by a team for the course Introduction to Game Design and Development.
